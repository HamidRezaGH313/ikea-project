Balcony Tables

Balcony tables are the perfect choice when you want to add some outdoor joy to your home. Whether you want to dine al fresco or just enjoy the sunshine, these tables are made from durable materials and offer a practical and stylish solution that can help you make the most of your outdoor space. The size of balcony tables usually depends on the size and shape of your balcony. For small balconies, a two-person balcony table and chair set consisting of balcony chairs and a balcony table is ideal. For larger balconies, you can choose a larger balcony table and chair set. Balcony tables can also have folding features so that they do not take up space when not in use and provide you with ease of storage.

Balcony Table Models

Balcony tables are a great way to make the most of a small outdoor space. They are designed to fit balconies or patios where traditional outdoor products may not fit. Balcony tables come in various shapes and sizes and you can choose from wooden balcony tables, plastic balcony tables or metal balcony tables. 

One of the biggest advantages of balcony tables is their versatility. You can use it as a dining table, a work table or even as a place to relax with a cup of coffee in the morning. Small balcony tables are designed in the form of a folding balcony table to save space.  
Another advantage of balcony tables is that, they’re easy to to assemble and use. Many balcony table models are lightweight and foldable, making them easy to transport and store when not in use. 

Wooden Balcony Tables

Wooden balcony tables are the perfect choice for those looking for a natural and rustic look. Wood creates a warm atmosphere on your balcony. In addition, wooden balcony tables are durable and long-lasting.  

Maintenance of wooden balcony tables is also important. The surface of wooden tables may wear or stains may occur over time. Therefore, the surface of the tables should be periodically coated with a special wood stain. It may also be useful to use a parasol or outdoor shades to protect the table from direct sunlight.

Folding Balcony Tables

Folding balcony tables, which are a great choice to increase the use of small balconies in apartments, are available in different sizes and styles. Folding balcony tables preferred for small balconies can be quickly folded and stored in a corner or hung on the wall when not in use, thus providing ease of storage on balconies.

You can save space by using folding balcony tables together with folding balcony chairs. In addition, folding balcony tables provide portability on the go and are also suitable for use on picnics, beaches or camping. You can also use it together with outdoor tables and outdoor chairs to create extra seating in your outdoor space. 

Hanging Balcony Tables

Another option for small balconies is hanging balcony tables. By mounting it on the balcony railing, you can create a single dining and outdoor area on your balcony.

Small balcony tables

Small balconies have the potential to utilise the outdoor space and create a pleasant seating area, despite having limited space. Small balcony tables designed for such balconies are important in terms of practicality and functionality. Small balconies are a valuable outdoor space for many people who are part of city life. However, it can be difficult to use and decorate these balconies due to limited space. At this point, furniture such as small balcony tables provide a great convenience. These balcony tables can attract attention with their compact design and practical functions. Small balcony tables not only provide a space for eating or drinking coffee, but also offer a great platform for displaying your plants and decorative items. With small accessories such as flower pots or decorative candles, you can personalize your balcony and create a pleasant atmosphere.

Metal balcony tables

Metal balcony tables are very easy to maintain. Metal surfaces are usually water-resistant and simple to clean. You can clean and polish the surface of the table using a mild soap solution with a cloth or a soft sponge. This practical maintenance routine ensures that the table remains long-lasting and eye-catching.
